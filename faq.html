Q: What is LAB Case Club - CERNO?

A: LAB Case Club, also known as CERNO, is a student organization at LAB University of Applied Sciences dedicated to developing professional skills through solving real-world business cases. We provide students with hands-on experience in strategic thinking, problem-solving, and business analysis through both internal practice sessions and collaboration with actual companies.

Q: How can I join the Case Club?

A: We welcome all motivated students who want to develop their professional skills! You can join by contacting us through the Contact page on our website or by reaching out to us on Instagram or LinkedIn. We hold regular meetings and events throughout the academic year, and new members are always welcome regardless of their experience level.

Q: Do I need prior experience with case competitions to participate?

A: Not at all! While prior experience is helpful, it's not required. We provide training and resources for beginners, and our team includes members with various levels of experience. Our internal practice sessions are designed to help you learn and improve your case-solving skills in a supportive environment before participating in larger competitions.

Q: What kind of events does CERNO organize?

A: We organize various events throughout the year, including case competition workshops, networking sessions with business professionals, participation in national and international case competitions, and career fairs like DuuniExpo. Check our Events page for the latest schedule and upcoming activities.

Q: How can my company collaborate with CERNO?

A: We're always looking for partnership opportunities! Companies can collaborate with us through sponsorships, providing real business cases for our students to solve, offering mentorship, or participating in our events. If you're interested in partnering with us, please visit our Contact page and fill out the business collaboration form, and we'll get back to you promptly.
